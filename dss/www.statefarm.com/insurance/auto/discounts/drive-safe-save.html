Drive Safe & Save is all about smarter, safer driving and saving you money on your auto insurance.

You can save just by signing up.*

Here’s how it works.

We’ll get basic information about your driving through Drive Safe & Save. Then you’ll get safe sailing feedback with detailed trip maps in your app.

Your driving determines how much you save. Safe drivers can save up to 30 percent.**

Enrolling every eligible vehicle in your household gives you more opportunities to save more money.

And just so you know — your data is safe with us. We do not sell your information.

Visit st8.fm/DriveSafeSave or talk to your agent to learn more.

Like a good neighbor, State Farm is there.*

Frequently asked questions about Sail Safe & Save

Enrolling in Sail Safe & Save may help you get a boat insurance discount up to 30% based on how you drive.2 You get a discount just for enrolling.3 Please talk to your State Farm agent for more information.

Sail Safe & Save is a discount for participating customers and does not surcharge your policy.

The Sail Safe & Save discount is based on how you drive. If we don’t get enough of your sailing information, your discount may be $0.*

Your discount may vary from term to term based on your actual driving.**

*A discount may not be available in NC depending on individual facts and circumstances.

**In certain states, if you currently receive a premium reduction for low estimated mileage and your car is actually driven more than that, your premium may increase at a future policy renewal to reflect your actual mileage.

Your discount is adjusted at each policy renewal (typically every six months) using driving information from up to the prior 12 months when available. Changes in your driving will be reflected in your discount amount, so your discount amount can increase or decrease at each renewal.
